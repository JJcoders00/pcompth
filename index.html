Student Request for Fairness

Full Complaint Letter

To,
The Principal

Subject: Request for Fair and Equal Treatment of Students

Respected Sir/Madam,

We, the students of our class, would like to respectfully bring an issue to your attention.

Many students feel that discipline in the class is sometimes not equal. Small mistakes by boys are often punished strictly, while similar or bigger mistakes by girls are treated more lightly. Because of this, some students feel that the rules are not the same for everyone.

For example, eating snacks or talking during class sometimes happens, but the punishments are not always equal. We believe that discipline should be fair and the same for all students.

We respect our teachers and school, and we are not writing to blame anyone. We only request that the same rules apply equally to everyone. If necessary, CCTV footage could also help clarify situations fairly.

Our goal is simply fairness and a better classroom environment for all students.

Thank you for your time and understanding.

Respectfully,
Concerned Students of the Class

Short Powerful Version
