Complaint to principal by Thomas Angelo Binto

To The Principal Respected: Request for Fair and Equal Treatment of Students
Respected/Madam, We, the students of our class, would like to respectfully bring an issue to your attention. Recently, many students have felt that disciplinary actions in our class are not always equal. Sometimes small mistakes made by boys are punished very strictly, while similar or bigger mistakes made by girls are treated more lightly. This has made some students feel that the rules are not the same for everyone. For example, there have been situations where some students eat snacks during class time or talk while the teacher is teaching. When boys make small mistakes, they are often scolded or punished strongly. However, when girls do similar things, the punishment is sometimes much lighter. We are not writing this complaint to blame anyone. We respect our teachers and our school. We only request that the same rules and discipline apply equally to all students, regardless of whether they are boys or girls. Some students also suggested that, if needed, the CCTV footage could help clarify situations so that decisions can be fair and based on facts. Our request is simply for equal treatment and fairness for everyone in the class.
